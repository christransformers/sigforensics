Professional summary

Lauren Morrell is a forensic document examiner with over 12 years of specialised experience in questioned document examination, handwriting comparison, and signature authentication. She holds Official Membership of The Australasian Society of Forensic Document Examiners (ASFDE), the principal professional body for forensic document examiners in Australasia.

Her casework spans civil litigation, criminal defence, probate disputes, familial provision claims, and fraud investigations. She regularly liaises with the Australian Federal Police and maintains active professional membership with ANZFSS. Lauren has provided expert opinion on hundreds of matters involving disputed handwriting, contested signatures, and document integrity.
