Professional summary

J. Brandi is a forensic document examiner with over 24 years of continuous practice in questioned document examination, handwriting and signature analysis, and forensic reporting. She serves as an independent consultant to the Australian Federal Police and is regularly accepted as an expert witness in Australian courts including the Supreme Court of NSW, the District Court, the Local Court, and the Federal Circuit and Family Court of Australia.

As Director of Signature & Handwriting Forensics Pty Ltd (trading as Signature Forensic), she leads a specialised forensic practice based in Manly, Sydney, providing court-admissible document examination services to legal practitioners, law enforcement, government agencies, and corporate clients nationally.
