    Comprehensive, seamless support on every aspect of migration law

    SignatureForensics Migration Services, an incorporated immigration legal practice, provides strategic, expert and practical advice in all aspects of visa and migration-related requirements. We offer a one-stop shop and a range of services on all aspects of immigration, nationality law and related matters.

    When you partner with us, you’ll have access to a team of Australia’s leading immigration lawyers – who bring a unique depth and breadth of knowledge on all aspects of immigration law, including employer-sponsored temporary and permanent entry (and related matters), labour agreements, business skills migration, skilled migration, family migration, student visas, merits and judicial review, international education, ministerial submissions, and citizenship.

    We specialise in complex migration, health and character cases.

    How SignatureForensics can help

    Our specialist team of immigration lawyers can assist you with the following:

    • Human resource strategy and review: design and implement human resource strategy and frameworks tailored to business needs and the enterprise generally. This encompasses controls, delegated responsibility, document management systems, and processes that align with immigration and human resources and include data protection, data privacy and monitoring.
    • Managing and responding to sponsorship, monitoring, compliance, investigations and enforcement: enabling the business to meet its support and related obligations, including reviewing and providing information and documents as relevant in each case.
    • Forensic investigations: rapid response to incidences of the breach including identification of cause and implementation of remediation measures as may be required.
    • Awareness, training and education: customised education and awareness programs for boards, senior management and staff.
    • Incident response planning: develop comprehensive reporting to minimise the impact of a breach. This considers business-critical processes, and the roles and responsibilities of relevant individuals in the organisation.

    We utilise the expertise and professionalism of our accomplished SignatureForensics global network to benefit our clients, including expatriate tax, global mobility and employment practices addressing all international mobility and human resource needs.

    We pride ourselves on knowing our clients' business, industry and operational needs, and represent individual, domestic and multinational corporations across various industry sectors. These include:

    • Tourism and hospitality
    • Building and construction
    • Information technology
    • Health
    • Education
    • Manufacturing
    • International education services
    • Retail
    • Biomedical
    • Mining.
