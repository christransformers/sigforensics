Types of Fraud We Investigate

From internal misconduct to complex external fraud schemes, our team has the experience to handle any investigation.

💰 Employee Fraud

Payroll manipulation, expense reimbursement fraud, procurement kickbacks, inventory theft, and ghost employee schemes. We identify internal control weaknesses and quantify losses.

📄 Financial Statement Fraud

Revenue recognition manipulation, asset overstatement, liability concealment, and related party transaction abuse. Our team works with auditors and regulators to detect and quantify misstatements.

🔗 Third-Party & Vendor Fraud

Supplier collusion, bid rigging, overcharging schemes, and shell company arrangements. We trace procurement transactions and identify irregular vendor relationships.

💻 Cyber-Enabled Fraud

Business email compromise (BEC), payment redirection fraud, CEO impersonation, and ransomware payment tracing. We work alongside cyber security experts to trace digital financial crime.

🏦 Asset Misappropriation

Theft of cash, inventory, or intellectual property. Our forensic accountants trace asset movements through complex corporate structures and identify diversion points.

⚖️ Regulatory & Compliance Fraud

AML/CTF breaches, sanctions evasion, bribery and corruption investigations. Our team supports regulatory responses and investigations by ASIC, AUSTRAC, and other bodies.

Our Investigation Methodology

Every engagement follows a structured, evidence-based methodology designed to withstand legal scrutiny.

1

Assessment & Planning

Initial case assessment, preservation of evidence, and development of an investigation plan tailored to your specific circumstances.

2

Data Collection & Preservation

Forensic acquisition of financial records, electronic data, and communications. Chain of custody maintained throughout.

3

Analysis & Investigation

Transaction testing, data analytics, interviews, and asset tracing to identify and quantify fraudulent activity.

4

Reporting & Testimony

Detailed forensic reports with findings, loss quantification, and expert witness testimony where required.
