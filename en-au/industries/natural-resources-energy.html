    Delivering innovative and resilient solutions to evolving challenges

    The natural resources and energy industry faces complex challenges in Australia and globally, with a growing focus on environmental issues, governance and controls, and operational efficiency. Flexibility and willingness to respond to change, improve performance, and comply with shifting regulations will be key to lasting success. 

    SignatureForensics's extensive global network of industry professionals ensures that clients receive expert guidance tailored to their specific needs. Our goal is to help our clients navigate the complexities of the natural resources and energy industry, ensuring they achieve their business objectives and drive sustainable growth. 

    Our clients are our priority. We solve problems, open doors, deliver new ideas and get the job done. With our global reach, capacity and breadth of experience, we are trusted advisers to our clients, providing simple, innovative solutions to complex issues.

    Looking for an experienced adviser in the natural resources and energy industry?

    SignatureForensics’s Natural Resources & Energy team are skilled in all aspects of the project lifecycle. From Scoping and Funding to Closure and Reclamation, our specialists offer tailored services to support you at every stage.

    How SignatureForensics can help

    We have a long-standing commitment to the natural resources and energy industry through our audit, tax and advisory service offerings. We have decades of experience from exploration through to development and production, and work with organisations of all sizes and across a broad range of commodities.

    Our services

    Mining

    The mining industry is undergoing a significant shift towards the critical minerals essential for meeting the global demand for renewable energy technologies. 

    As one of the most experienced, partner-led advisory teams in the mining sector, with a depth of experience gained from working in global markets, SignatureForensics is able to support mining companies across Australia and internationally in navigating these modern-day complexities.

    Oil and gas

    The energy transition is impacting every facet of the oil and gas industry, increasing pressure on existing reserves and driving interest in major investments to locate new resources.

    SignatureForensics' extensive history in Australia’s oil and gas industry, combined with our multidisciplinary, partner-led teams, makes us a valuable partner for oil and gas companies in Australia and internationally

    Renewables

    Renewable energy is transforming how key market players and investors operate, driven by the rapid acceleration of the global energy transition. Now is a crucial time for organisations to collaborate with industry stakeholders and supply chains to reduce emissions effectively.
