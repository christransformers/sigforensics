    Specialised support for the automotive industry

    The automotive industry is evolving. Electric and autonomous vehicles, increased connectivity within vehicles, changing customer trends and the need to reduce emissions presents manufacturers and dealers with numerous opportunities and challenges. SignatureForensics is a specialised automotive service provider assisting franchised dealers, manufacturers and industry associations with a wide range of financial and consulting services.

    How SignatureForensics can help

    We work with clients across all aspects of the automotive sector in Australia, supporting motor vehicle, truck, agricultural, caravan, marine equipment and motorcycle dealerships. Our focus is on key business issues and operational areas, while giving you constructive ideas to drive the growth of your dealership.

    Talk to us about how we can help your business.

    Our team has extensive hands-on experience in:

    • Dealership tax planning and compliance 
    • Buy/sell due diligence, valuations, and mergers and acquisitions
    • Audit and assurance services
    • Dealer accountant training
    • Family business and succession planning
    • Strategic planning, operational reviews, profitability enhancement and benchmarking services
    • Remuneration reviews, superannuation planning and people management.
