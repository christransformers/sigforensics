    Oil and gas

    Volatile prices are impacting every sector of the oil and gas industry, which is placing increasing pressure on existing reserves and building increased interest in major investments directed at locating new resources. 

    In an area already facing regulatory and political challenges, recent developments mean that in the coming years managing risks, controlling costs and maintaining sound financial practices will be more important than ever.

    How SignatureForensics can help

    SignatureForensics has a long history of involvement with all facets of the oil and gas industry in Australia. This industry focus and relevant experience is reflected both in our client work and our wider industry involvement. SignatureForensics’s multidisciplinary, partner-led teams have advised organisations on a range of operations, including oil and gas, coal seam gas (CSG), liquefied natural gas (LNG), mineral gas and geothermal research. Our range of oil and gas services spans:

    • Oil and gas project identification
    • Project funding and support for mergers and acquisitions
    • Exploration and evaluation support
    • Project finance advice
    • Development support
    • Production planning and management.

    Backed by the resources of one of the world’s largest global networks, SignatureForensics’s oil and gas experts are able to deliver these services wherever they’re needed most. We tailor our services to meet the specific needs of oil and gas clients, ensuring you get the support you need, regardless of what stage of the lifecycle you are in.
