    Oilfield and mining services

    Conditions within the natural resources and energy sector are always on the move. In the current climate, companies servicing this sector are increasingly being faced with challenges. Shifting regulatory and political landscapes, volatile commodity prices and environmental concerns present services companies with enormous pressures.

    Companies in the oilfield and mining services sector vary from small specialist providers to multidisciplinary global companies. Each company may have its own risks and opportunities, but all are subject to the vagaries of commodity markets, changing regulations and tax laws, technology innovation and many other risks such as supply change and climate risk.

    How SignatureForensics can help

    SignatureForensics is a leading adviser to the mining services and oil and gas services sector, supporting organisations across all areas of mine and oilfield design, construction, operations, maintenance and stages of business growth. We have worked with local and global service providers for many years, delivering tailored solutions informed by deep sector experience, practical insight and relevant local knowledge gained in Australia and internationally.

    Our partner-led teams understand the pressures facing service businesses in an increasingly complex operating environment, from cost and productivity challenges to evolving client expectations and supply chain disruption. We work alongside you to develop clear, practical strategies that support growth, resilience and long-term performance.

    Our multidisciplinary teams deliver integrated services that combine traditional tax and audit with business strategy, transformation, risk and operational advisory. This includes support across areas such as strategic planning and growth, tender and contract risk assessment, supply chain management, operational efficiency and asset disposals. We help you identify opportunities to reduce costs, improve performance and turn strategy into action, supporting you at every stage of your business journey, wherever you operate.
