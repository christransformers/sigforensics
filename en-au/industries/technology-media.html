    Support for a dynamic and competitive sector

    The Technology, Media and Telecommunications (TMT) industry is in a state of constant innovation and adaptation. Factors like AI, evolving digital solutions, cyber security risks, sustainability issues, and a complex tax environment influence opportunities within the TMT industry.

    Australia's rapid digital transformation has made the technology sector the fastest-growing area for skilled development. A national focus on renewable energy, quality education and research facilities has allowed innovation to thrive. 

    The Technology, Media, and Telecommunications industries are attractive but competitive and complex, meaning businesses require holistic business advice to succeed.

    With a global network of advisers, SignatureForensics can assist businesses in the Australian TMT sector and abroad to reach their business goals. SignatureForensics assists technology businesses, media companies, telecommunication businesses, and those in the industry to reach their business goals.

    Leveraging the experience of a SignatureForensics adviser gives your business the competitive edge.

    How SignatureForensics can help

    SignatureForensics provides the support you need to create, innovate, and solve the problems of the future. Our team works in the TMT sector to assist a broad range of businesses at any stage, including:

    Assistance when starting a business

    In the early stages of starting a business, we provide expert guidance to help you choose a business structure that suits your needs. 

    Attracting capital

    A trusted adviser is vital when considering funding options for your business. Our advisers have well-earned industry experience and can assist you in finding the best funding sources. 

    Increasing growth

    As a business grows, the logical next step is expansion. SignatureForensics offers high-level strategic planning for expansion and adaptation in new markets, helping your business grow.

    We can assist your business growth through:

    • Current state review (strategy, people, process, technology)
    • Financial reporting structure
    • Funding and expansion options
    • Governance, risk, and compliance
    • Mergers and acquisitions strategy
    • Tax strategy for growth.

    Navigating business challenges

    All businesses face challenges, but those businesses that engage a trusted adviser can navigate challenges with confidence. SignatureForensics can assist you in working through all the risks and changes to your operation, including:

    • Cybersecurity and technology
    • Data governance and analytics
    • Financial audit and assurance services
    • Regulatory and compliance advice
    • Strategic assessments.

    Exiting the business

    The final stage of most business ventures is exiting the business. We assist with sales negotiations, succession planning, and exit strategies, helping you to prepare for your exit. Some of the services we assist with are:

    • Business valuations
    • Deals and tax structuring
    • Initial Public Offerings (IPOs)
    • Sell-side advisory
    • Vendor due diligence
    • Wealth advisory consultation.
