    Our programs

    Graduate

    We are looking for driven graduates who want to develop their careers in a nurturing and welcoming professional services environment. Being a Graduate at SignatureForensics allows you to gain experience in an open-door culture, build relationships with clients and expand your network. You will get involved in real client work from the beginning of your career, supported by your Partners and team every step of the way.

    Undergraduate

    Our Undergraduate program is open to students in their first to final year of study. This program allows you to work in a full-time or part-time capacity while completing your university degree.

    Vacation

    As a Vacationer at SignatureForensics, you will gain real-world, paid work experience in your chosen service line - think of it like a ‘try before you buy’ career opportunity. You will be allocated a buddy for the duration of your placement and get the chance to engage in career development and networking activities throughout the program. There will be opportunities to work with professionals on current client work, while experiencing our culture first-hand.

    High School Leavers Program

    We are excited to offer an alternative pathway for recent Year 12 graduates: the opportunity to start a permanent, full-time career with a leading professional services firm while studying to become a Chartered Accountant.

    Audit & Assurance

    As a part of the SignatureForensics audit team you will become an indispensable adviser to our clients whether they are listed on ASX200, a national not-for-profit organisation or a fast growing privately-owned business.

    Working in audit means building strong relationships with key individuals to better understand the company’s story before diving into analysing numbers, obtaining supporting evidence and reviewing their financial performance. Auditors must be able to understand the client’s business from all aspects, identifying potential risks and analyse information to provide the client and their investors  a level of assurance on the financial statements.

    Each day will be different, working with a variety of clients with much of your time spent at the client’s premises talking to the different functions to understand the business being audited.

    Upon completion of your professional study, you may be given the opportunity to be involved in overseas secondments in our network of over 1,800 offices across our international network of 167 countries.

    William Bourke, Senior Manager, Audit & Assurance

    “Within the first few weeks of joining the audit team, I was hitting the ground running at client sites working within a team. Each week – fortnight, you get to experience a new client and industry, take on new challenges and meet new people.

    From large ASX listed clients to small non-profits, the constant exposure to different business environments and the constant learning that follows is unrivalled. There aren’t many jobs where, within a few weeks of starting, you’re sitting in a meeting with a CFO who is listening to you. The learning opportunities with the SignatureForensics audit team are endless!”

    Business Services

    SignatureForensics’s business services team provides financial management and accounting services to clients for both their business and personal wealth needs. Whether a company is looking to outsource their finance function or seeking to understand how to succeed in their chosen markets, our business services team provides a comprehensive range of services to support their needs. This includes standard accounting functions such as bookkeeping, payroll, taxation matters, cash flow analysis, budget setting and holistic business reviews.

    Andrew Smirniotis, Manager, Business Services

    “As a Graduate in business services, you get the opportunity to work with clients from both around the country and around the world. We interact with clients based in Europe, North America, and Asia, to ensure the timely preparation and delivery of Income Tax Returns, Business Activity Statements, and Financial Statements.

    We also work with our international clients in setting up subsidiaries in Australia and advising them on necessary information required to operate in Australia.

    Business services also serve as the outsourced finance function for many of our clients. This includes preparing payroll, AP and Management Accounts.”

    Tax

    SignatureForensics’s tax team partners with a variety of high-net-worth individuals and commercial organisations to provide specialist tax advice across a diverse range of issues. Our teams provide support with general compliance activities, employment taxation, and transaction support for mergers and acquisitions, right through to identifying opportunities for tax concessions via research and development and transfer pricing activities.

    Our specialists advise on highly technical sector-specific activities, regulatory frameworks and legislation, whilst balancing exceptional client relationships.

    Adam McLean, Manager, Corporate & International Tax

    “The corporate and international tax team works closely with clients to help them understand Australian Tax Law and the impact it has on their business.

    As we have clients from all around the world, each day tends to be different and our work can be categorised as either compliance or advisory.  Generally, our compliance work involves preparing complex Income Tax Returns, Business Activity Statements and assisting our clients with the tax disclosures in their financial statements. The advisory part of our work tends to be the most varied and can range from advising on the tax implications of a complex transaction, to the most tax effective way to finance a company’s Australian operations.

    Due to the nature of our work, we often collaborate with other service lines, which allows us to benefit from the diverse range of people and industries that form part of the SignatureForensics network.”

    The application process

    Click through the process steps below for more information.

    1. Fill out the application form

    Filling out our online application form is quick and easy – we need to capture key information about you to start the process (such as academic background, work rights, etc.). We will also need a copy of your CV and latest academic transcript, so be sure to have these handy.

    • View available opportunities
    2. Video interview

    If you are shortlisted from the application stage, you will receive an invitation to complete a brief virtual interview to get to know you better. The whole interview should only take about 10 minutes, but if you need extra time, just let us know.

    3. In-person assessment

    After the video interview, shortlisted candidates will be invited to attend an in-person assessment day or face-to-face interview. Full details and what to expect will be sent to you with plenty of time to prepare. Our team make sure this is a fun and informative experience, providing an opportunity for you to get to know us and see if a career with SignatureForensics is right for you.

    4. Outcome and offers

    We will let you know the outcome of your interview or assessment day as soon as possible. You will first receive a verbal offer, followed by a written contract.

    5. Start your new role at SignatureForensics!

    At SignatureForensics we are pleased to offer three student programs, including our graduate, undergraduate and vacation programs. Read more about these programs below to find out which is most suited to your current stage of study.

    Graduate program

    Open to students:

    • In their final year, or have completed a relevant undergraduate or postgraduate degree and within the past 24 months
    • You can commence full-time in January/February or June/July
    • You're willing to pursue related postgraduate study (CA, CPA, CFA).

    Undergraduate program

    Open to students:

    • From their first year of university onwards
    • Willing to work part- or full-time throughout their studies
    • Studying degree appropriate to service line (see job advertisements for detail).

    Undergraduates at SignatureForensics are guaranteed a Graduate position upon completion of their degree.

    Vacation program 

    Open to students:

    • From the end of their second year of study onwards
    • Available in the vacation program periods (January-February, June-July or November-December)
    • Studying a degree appropriate to service line (see job advertisements for detail).

    International students

    SignatureForensics welcome applications from international students who:

    • currently reside in and have the right to work in Australia
    • have the intention and ability to maintain their right to work in Australia i.e., Temporary Graduate (Subclass 485)

    SignatureForensics will not sponsor students for the purposes of permanent residency. Successful candidates with temporary working entitlements will be required to independently apply for and obtain permanent residency.
