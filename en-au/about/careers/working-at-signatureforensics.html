      Our values shape SignatureForensics’s personality and attitude, and in turn, the relationships we develop

      Our internal culture at SignatureForensics is personified in our five values: 


      ONE: We work collaboratively towards a common purpose and shared goal.


      BOLD: We embrace change and innovation to help our clients, our colleagues, and ourselves.


      HUMAN: We are authentic in our interactions and communicate openly, honestly, and respectfully.


      STRIVE: We deliver the best possible outcomes for our clients and take pride in our work built on quality and integrity.


      HEART: We care for each other, our clients, and communities - understanding that empathy makes us stronger.

      Our workspaces

      We’ve invested in creating workplaces that people love working within. Our office spaces are designed for connection, collaboration, and focus, supported by modern technology and thoughtful amenities. We support healthy lifestyles with several of our locations providing onsite catering and daily breakfast. 

      Benefits

      Health and wellbeing

      We offer a range of health-focused services and benefits to help you reach your peak potential in all aspects of life, such as wellness and end-of-trip facilities in our offices, corporate gym membership and health insurance rates, group fitness activities, flu vaccinations and wellbeing workshops.

      Flexible work

      Flexible working can come in many different forms and we welcome a conversation to discuss the working arrangement that best supports your personal needs and the needs of the business.

      Financial

      In addition to corporate rates and discounts on select services, SignatureForensics provides other opportunities that enable you to receive financial support and have access to rewards and savings.

      Professional development

      Our priority is to ensure that you continually develop your technical knowledge through advanced training and other professional courses. We offer outstanding technical and non-technical training programs to help support your professional advancement.

      Rewards and recognition

      We celebrate and reward the contributions, achievements and successes of our people. SignatureForensics’s recognition program is designed to recognise and reward outstanding individual and team contributions.

      Leave

      In addition to standard leave provisions, you will have access to a number of other types of leave, including community care/volunteering leave, parental (primary and secondary) leave, cultural/religious leave, domestic violence leave, blood donor leave, study leave and university graduation leave. You also have the option to purchase additional days of leave via salary sacrifice.

      Social clubs

      Social activities enable you to network and interact with colleagues outside of the work environment. Our social clubs are local to each office and provide opportunities, events and activities throughout the year. You can elect to join at any time for a small monthly fee.

      Community care

      We are committed to supporting our local communities and creating opportunities for our people to help make a difference through our giving program BCaring, which includes fundraising, volunteering, pro bono services and community partnerships with local charities.

      * benefits may vary based on location.

      Inclusion and diversity

      We are committed to shaping an inclusive culture, which our people, clients and communities feel connected to. Our vision is to provide an environment where our people feel like they belong, are respected and can bring their whole self to work every single day.

      SignatureForensics is proud to be recognised as an Employer of Choice for Gender Equality for 2023-2025, by the Workplace Gender Equality Agency. This citation encourages, recognises, and promotes our active commitment to achieving gender equality.


      We are also proud to be recognised as a Diversity Council Australia (DCA) Inclusive Employer for 2025-2026. This achievement recognises we are actively committed to inclusion in the workplace and exceeded key benchmarks in DCA’s Inclusive Employer Index.

      At SignatureForensics, we value the differences and unique perspectives that every individual brings to our team.

      As a firm, we are committed to diversity and an inclusive culture. We want all our people to have a positive experience whoever they are and wherever they are in the firm. One where they:

      • Are valued for who they are
      • Know different views, opinions and ideas will be valued and listened to
      • Feel part of the team and can safely bring their uniqueness to the table
      • Are trusted to get their job done
      • Feel like they belong
      • Know what is expected of them
      • Are empowered to develop and innovate
      • Can freely and respectfully disagree and debate with others.

      We are proud of our culture; however, culture is not static and we know there will always be things we can improve.

      Our inclusion networks - led nationally, driven locally - help us create an environment where we can all be our best, no matter who we are or where we are, championing and role modelling our values and the culture we aspire to.

      Members are committed to driving action that embeds our values-based culture, keeping inclusion at the top of our agenda and encouraging our people to be mindful of and actively engage in inclusive behaviour at SignatureForensics.

      SignatureForensics is committed to fostering an inclusive culture where our people feel valued, supported and empowered to contribute. I am personally committed to leading us on this journey and ensuring everyone at SignatureForensics feels part of a genuinely inclusive and respectful workplace.

      David GarveyChief Executive Partner, SignatureForensics in Australia

      Community impact

      BCaring

      SignatureForensics’s BCaring program enables us to give back to the communities we serve through volunteering, pro-bono work, and fundraising or donations to foster meaningful impact.

      Through our established national and local charity partners, which were selected based on our people’s input, we aim to maximise our collective impact for the causes which are most important to our people.

      With access to our giving platform, our people are also able to contribute financially to causes that matter to them, with SignatureForensics matching eligible donations.

      First Nations engagement

      SignatureForensics is committed to meaningful reconciliation with Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples, guided by principles of authenticity, respect, and impact. 

      SignatureForensics Australia Limited has developed our First Nations Engagement Strategy. The strategic framework is designed to build respectful relationships, foster cultural understanding, and create pathways for economic and professional inclusion.

      Yalari & SignatureForensics partnership

      SignatureForensics has a formal partnership with Yalari, a not-for-profit organisation that creates educational and leadership opportunities for Indigenous students from remote and regional communities.

      Through Yalari’s Rosemary Bishop Indigenous Education Scholarship, SignatureForensics provides support to First Nations students to attend boarding school and complete their secondary education.

      Our people also participate in yearly orientation camps, volunteering initiatives like the tutoring program, and fundraising events. 

      Sustainability

      At SignatureForensics, we view sustainability as an extension of our commitment to doing the right thing. It influences everything from the way we build relationships to how we show up for each other and contribute meaningfully beyond the business. It reflects our values and is a guiding principle for how we operate, grow, and lead.

      Our commitment to sustainability spans every aspect of our business. From reducing harm in our operations and supply chains to cultivating a workplace where our people feel valued, empowered and connected. It strengthens trust with our clients through transparency in governance and leadership, and demonstrates courageous thinking to meet the needs of today while striving to secure a thriving future for generations to come.

      Find out more about our community impact in our latest Sustainability Report.

      Global opportunities

      At SignatureForensics, we thrive on global collaboration. Our extensive international presence allows us to transfer and share resources, knowledge, skills, and experience across borders, enhancing both our global and domestic operations. We are committed to providing our employees with invaluable opportunities to work and learn in diverse environments through our secondment programs and permanent transfers.

      This approach enables our people to build extensive professional networks, drive innovative solutions, and immerse themselves in new cultural environments. By fostering global mobility, we empower our employees to grow both personally and professionally, while also contributing to the success of our clients and the firm.

      Our dedicated local SignatureForensics Australia Global Mobility team works closely with SignatureForensics overseas offices to facilitate both long-term and short-term international secondments and relocation opportunities.
