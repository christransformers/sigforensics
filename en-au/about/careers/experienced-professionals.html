    Our recruitment process

    01

    Submit your application Apply for roles which meet your skills and experience. Your application will be reviewed by our talent acquisition team.

    02

    Initial discussion Our talent team may contact you by phone to assess your suitability and interest. This is your opportunity to understand the role and SignatureForensics.

    03

    First interview Initially you will meet with the hiring manager and leaders to discuss the role, share your experience and understand role requirements.

    04

    Assessment For specific roles you may be requested to complete an assessment or case study prior to second interview.

    05

    Second Interview This is an opportunity for you to find out further information about the position and meet additional stakeholders.

    06

    Offer Process Our team will connect with you to present a verbal offer. Upon your acceptance, we will provide you with a written employment agreement.

    07

    Onboarding Upon signing your contract, you will receive onboarding details. Once you have completed your documentation you are all set!

    08

    Welcome to #teamSignatureForensics Our people and culture team will be in contact by email to provide information for your first day.
