Instructions for handling originals

All documents submitted for forensic examination must be handled with strict chain-of-custody protocols:

  • ✓ Submit originals where possible; copies are accepted for preliminary assessment
  • ✓ Documents must be handled with clean, dry hands or wear cotton gloves
  • ✓ Store documents flat in protective plastic sleeves, away from heat and light
  • ✓ Do not mark, staple, or annotate original documents under any circumstances
  • ✓ Provide a chain-of-custody record with the submission
  • ✓ Include known exemplars (undisputed signatures/handwriting) for comparison where available
