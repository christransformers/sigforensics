    Executive Summary

    Executive summary

    Ben is the Partner in Charge of SignatureForensics’s Tax team in Melbourne, and SignatureForensics’s National Leader of Global Expatriate & Employment Taxes and Leader of Payroll Advisory. He helps his clients navigate a broad range of people, tax, pay, and reward issues, and specialises in the unique employment, human resources (HR), finance, and compliance challenges that Australian complex employment tax, industrial relations, and superannuation legislation present.

    Ben also focuses on the multinational compliance and remuneration challenges associated with employee movement between countries, and has over 20 years’ experience in expatriate tax across Europe, Asia, and Australia.

    He has extensive experience across the retail, consumer products, construction, engineering, and hospitality sectors.

    Expertise

    • Cross-border personal and employment tax planning, strategy, and compliance
    • Fringe benefits tax compliance and consulting
    • Fair work, modern award, and EBA compliance management and remediation support
    • Employer payroll systems, obligations, and management
    • M&A due diligence reviews and reports
    • Mobility, tax, and HR policy and process design and implementation
    • Effective use of analytics and automation across tax, mobility, and HR
    • Workforce strategy consulting
    • Broad-based and executive remuneration consulting, including STI and LTI design, build, and implementation

    Experience

    • Multinational enterprises and organisations across Retail, Hospitality, Resources, Engineering and NFP sectors 
    • Significant experience working with the ATO, State Revenue Offices and Fair Work Ombudsman.
